AP Art History Art Finder

AP Art History Finder

Collapse
Overview

This was my first ever automation project that I came up with. To give context, I was taking an AP Art History class at the time. In the AP test for that class, students had to memorize 250 art pieces from different time periods. To help us memorize the art pieces, my teacher assigned an art journal where you had to print all 250 art pieces and write information about them. I appreciated that she wanted to help us, but I did not want to take the time to search through and print every single art piece. So, I wrote a script that searched up all 250 art pieces and saved them all to a pdf file. This saved me hours of time, and I also shared my script with the class so they could save time as well.

Time Spent

2 days. This time I took to write the script could have been spent to actually search up and print the art pieces manually, but I feel that it was worth it because I helped my classmates save many hours of time too. My python skills also progressed because of this, so I was satisfied.
