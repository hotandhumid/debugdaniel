Debug Daniel

Debug Daniel

Collapse
Overview

Debug Daniel (this website) is completely made from html, css, and js. It is a portfolio website for me that displays various kinds of information about me and my skills. The is the most difficult project with html that I have worked on. It was also definitely the longest one too. But, I had a ton of ideas of where to go with this so I was excited. The hardest part of this project was making the 3D version of my head to be close to perfect. I modeled my head with a 3D developing tool called Blender. I then had to figure out how to put it on my website, and make it spin. I searched around and found this JavaScript library called ThreeJS that can display 3D graphics, and also animate them. So, I learned it in about a week and set up everything. The second hardest part of this project, was remembering all of my past projects and trying to find the files on my computer. For my Photoshop, FCPX, and Premier projects, I had them stored on a separate computer where I had to scavenge for the files. But, in the end, I got most of everything to work and look like how I imagined it.

Time Spent

A span of 4 months. I started this in the summer of my junior year and was working on it almost everyday up until the start of my senior year, where I was busy with school.
